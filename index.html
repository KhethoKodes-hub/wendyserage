    OUR SERVICES

    Empowering Communities Through Agriculture

    01

    Youth Mentorship

    We take unemployed young people and transform them into confident, self-sustaining farmers and agri-entrepreneurs.

    02

    Practical Training

    Monthly on-farm training sessions open to youth and farmers from all nine provinces — free spots available every session.

    03

    Cabbage Production

    Commercial-scale cabbage farming delivering fresh, quality produce to markets — the backbone of Serage Holdings.

    04

    Agro-Processing

    We guide young people to go beyond farming into agro-processing businesses, adding value to raw produce.

    05

    Student Placements

    We host agriculture students from colleges like Tompi Seleka, Letaba, and Vhembe TVET for real-world farm experience.

    06

    Fresh Vegetables

    Supplying fresh, sustainably grown cabbages to markets, families, and local crèches in and around Limpopo.

    07

    Sustainable Farming

    Practising responsible agriculture that protects the land for future generations while producing quality food today.

    08

    Community Giving

    Donating fresh cabbages to Kids Kidos, Noko Crèche, and Mami's Crèche — because every child deserves healthy food.

    Why Choose Serage

    From humble beginnings to a nationally recognised agricultural hub

    Wendy Moshakga started Serage Holdings in 2021 with no land, no money, and no formal training — only determination. Today, Serage Farms trains youth from all nine provinces, hosts students and graduates, and is a beacon of hope for rural communities.

    Empowering Youth & Women

    Teaching practical farming and business skills to those who need it most.

    Community at the Core

    Supporting local crèches, guesthouses, shops, and transport — farming that lifts everyone.

    Fresh Produce, Real Impact

    Quality cabbages from Mbobo, Polokwane — grown with care, sold with pride.

    Farming Makes Lives Better

    At Serage Holdings, we believe agriculture is not just about survival — it's a career path that brings dignity, creates jobs, and builds communities. Our monthly training sessions attract participants from across South Africa and beyond, turning our farm into a hub of knowledge and economic activity.

    • Mentored 3 young men who now run their own farms
    • Trained 30 youth at Regen College — 2 launched businesses within 2 weeks
    • Currently hosting 20 students and graduates on-farm
